Who are we?

Exploring the depths, engineering the future!

UiASub is a student organization at the University of Agder bringing together students interested in technology, innovation and maritime development.

We work on projects in underwater robotics and modern subsea technology.

Through interdisciplinary collaboration we explore solutions for underwater robotics and contemporary subsea systems.

K2 ROV

Our new ROV

This is our new and improved K2 ROV. Eight vectored thrusters enable six degrees of freedom. Our power unit makes the ROV suitable for longer subsea missions while still delivering peak performance.

Expected production completed by 2026

After careful consideration we moved from three housings to a single central pressure vessel due to buoyancy challenges. As the radius increased, imbalance between buoyancy and weight became more apparent. To resolve this we chose a more compact design. Despite this change, the ROV's core functionality and internal components remain reliable and effective.
