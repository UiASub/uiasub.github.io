About UiASub

Student-driven underwater technology

UiASub is a student-driven technology group at the University of Agder. We build autonomous and remotely operated underwater systems, learn through hands-on projects, and collaborate closely with the industry.

Join us!

Exploring the depths, engineering the future

UiASub is a student-run organization dedicated to advancing subsea technology and exploration. Our mission is to encourage innovation, collaboration and education in marine technology and autonomous systems.

The organization runs like a company and provides valuable hands-on experience for your future career. We're looking for engineers, HR and management talent, marketing and media students, and more.

TAC Challenge

Where concept meets reality

An international ROV/AUV competition for students from around the world. The competition focuses on developing innovative solutions for subsea operations and gives participants the opportunity to test their skills in realistic scenarios.

2024 Missions:

  • Subsea Docking - Autonomous docking with power transfer
  • Pipeline Inspection - ArUco marker identification
  • Visual Inspection - Underwater structure inspection
  • Valve Intervention - Valve operation on the seabed
