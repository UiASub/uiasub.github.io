About UiASub

Student-driven underwater technology

UiASub is a student-driven technology group at the University of Agder. We build autonomous and remotely operated underwater systems, learn through practical projects, and collaborate closely with industry.

K2 ROV

Our new and improved ROV

This is our new ROV K2. 8 vectorized thrusters allow the ROV to move with 6 degrees of freedom. Our Power Unit makes the ROV perfect for longer underwater missions while still delivering maximum power.
