About UiASub

UiASub is a student-driven technology group at the University of Agder. We build autonomous and remotely operated underwater systems, learn through hands-on projects, and collaborate closely with the industry.

The organization's purpose is to offer students work-related projects focused on building remotely operated and autonomous underwater equipment, such as ROVs and AUVs. The organization operates like a company and provides students with practical experience that is valuable for their future careers. Being part of UiASub increases your attractiveness to employers and provides access to an extensive professional network. We participate in 1-2 competitions each year, where we compete against different teams to be the fastest at completing tasks on the seabed.
