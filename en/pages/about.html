About UiASub

UiASub is a student-run technology group at the University of Agder. We design, build and operate remotely operated and autonomous underwater systems, offering students hands-on project experience and close industry collaboration.

  • We provide project-based learning focused on ROVs and AUVs, building practical skills relevant to engineering careers.
