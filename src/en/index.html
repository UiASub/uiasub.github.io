K2 ROV

Our new ROV

This is our new and improved ROV K2. 8 vectorized thrusters allow the ROV to move with 6 degrees of freedom. Our Power Unit makes the ROV perfect for longer underwater missions, while still being able to deliver maximum power.

Expected to be produced by 2026

After careful consideration, we have gone from three containers to a single central container due to buoyancy issues. As the container's radius increased, the imbalance between buoyancy and weight became more apparent. To solve this, we have chosen a more compact design. Despite this change, the ROV's core functionality and internal components remain equally reliable and efficient.
