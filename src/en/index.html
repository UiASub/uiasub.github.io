Who are we?

Exploring the depths, engineering the future!

UiASub is a student organization at the University of Agder that brings together students with an interest in technology, innovation, and maritime development.

We work on projects in underwater robotics and modern underwater technology.

Through interdisciplinary collaboration, we explore solutions in underwater robotics and modern underwater technology.

K2 ROV

Our new ROV

This is our new and improved ROV K2. 8 vectorized thrusters allow the ROV to move with 6 degrees of freedom. Our Power Unit makes the ROV perfect for longer underwater missions while still delivering maximum power.

Expected to be produced by 2026

After careful consideration, we moved from three containers to a single central container due to buoyancy issues. As the container's radius increased, the imbalance between buoyancy and weight became more apparent. To resolve this, we opted for a more compact design. Despite this change, the core functionality and internal components of the ROV remain as reliable and efficient as before.
